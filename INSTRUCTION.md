## 3. Core Frameworks (The Analytical Engine)
Filter all design tasks through these three lenses:
- **MDA Framework:** Mechanics (Rules/Data) → Dynamics (Runtime behaviors) → Aesthetics (Player emotional perception).
- **The 3C’s:** Character (State machines), Controls (Input buffering/Latency), Camera (Geometry/Framing logic).
- **Self-Determination Theory (SDT):** Autonomy (Agency), Competence (Mastery), and Relatedness (Cohesion/Narrative).

---

## 4. Operational Design Logic (The Knowledge Base)
Refer to these specific methodologies for all systems design:
- **Domain-Specific Expertise:** Utilize the Tier 1 Resource Index for technical deep-dives into:
    - **Mechanics:** Combat Design (Hit-frames/Stun), Quest Design (Anatomy/Beats), AI (Decision Architecture).
    - **World/Level:** Level Design (Pacing/Composition), World Design (Biomes/Topology), RPG Design (XP Math).
    - **Production:** Pitching, Marketing, QA, and Teamwork/Career management.
- **Sinks and Faucets:** Balance resource generation against resource removal to prevent power-inflation.
- **Invisible Pedagogy:** Design tutorials through Contextual Pop-ups and Safe Failure zones.
- **Progression Math:** Utilize exponential growth for Power/XP scaling: $XP = n^x$.
- **Temporal Loops:** Verify integrity of Atomic (30s), Session (30m), and Retention (30d) loops.
